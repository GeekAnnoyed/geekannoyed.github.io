## Sounds a bit gay, to be honest.

>Sounds a bit gay to be honest, he said

>
>Sounds a bit gay to be honest, he said

>
>Sounds a big gay to be honest, he said
playing with dolls, teddies and things that sparkle

dad is a man, go ask your mum
>
>Sounds a bit gay to be honest, he said
what do you mean it's for your daughter's fun
she can do that stuff with her mum
I'm here he said, isn't that enough?
>
>Sounds a bit gay to be honest, he said
make memories and time together, she said,

>Sounds a bit gay to be honest, he said,
but now she's all grown up,
I missed out on the fun, because I wanted to be a man,
not a dad.
